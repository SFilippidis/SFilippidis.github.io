Academic education


[2008] Ph.D. from the department of Informatics, School of Sciences, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (Greece). Research area: New Educational Technologies. Ph.D. thesis title: Internet Based Education using Adaptive Hypermedia Systems. Grade: Excellent. Supervisor: professor I. A. Tsoukalas.

[2002] 2-year multi-departmental M.Sc. in Medical Informatics, departments of Medicine, Informatics, and Electrical and Computer Engineering, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (Greece). Grade: Excellent. Diploma thesis: Detecting non-stationarity in biomedical signals. Supervisor: lecturer L. Hadjileontiadis, department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Polytechnic School, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (Greece).

[2000] 4-year B.Sc. from the department of Informatics, School of Sciences, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (Greece). Grade: Very Good. Specialization of studies: Digital Media. Diploma thesis: Digital image watermarking using fractals and chaotic techniques, supervisor: professor I. Pitas.

[1996] 4-year B.Sc. from the department of Mathematics, School of Sciences, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (Greece). Grade: Very Good. Specialization of studies: Applied Mathematics.

Professional and teaching experience


[2001 - now] High school Teacher of Informatics and Mathematics with technical and general high schools at Katerini (Greece).

[2010] Adjunct Lecturer with the department of Informatics, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (Greece), teaching Human Computer Interaction (spring semester, academic year 2009-2010).

[2009] Adjunct Lecturer with the department of Philosophy and Pedagogy, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (Greece), teaching Informatics (spring semester, academic year 2008-2009).

[2005 - 2009] Laboratory Collaborator (2005-2006) and Seconded High School Teacher (2006-2009) with the department of Logistics at Katerini's branch of the Technological Educational Institute of Thessaloniki (Greece), teaching courses in the area of a) Information Technology's Applications for Logistics, b) Didactics.

[2003 - 2008] Laboratory Collaborator with the department of Informatics of the School of Technological Applications of the Technological Educational Institute of Thessaloniki (Greece), teaching theory (academic year 2003 – 2004) and lab (academic years 2004-2005, 2005-2006, 2006-2007 and 2007-2008) of the course "Digital Signal and Image Processing" of the 7th semester of studies.

[1998 - 2005] Teacher/Instructor of Informatics with several public and private institutes of professional training.

[1999 - 2000] Instructor and Tutor of Informatics with Mediterranean - ECS in courses for Higher National Diploma in Computing - Business Information Technology, teaching: Computer Platforms, Systems Analysis, Management Science, and in courses for Higher National Diploma in Information Technology, teaching: Programming Methods I & II, Systems Analysis, Operating Systems, Computer Architecture.

[1997 - 1998] Military Service in telecommunications’ branch of the Greek army (18 months).
